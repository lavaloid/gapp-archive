Genuinely Approachable Pencil Puzzles

Genuinely Approachable Pencil Puzzles (GAPP) is a daily series of pencil puzzles hosted on the Cracking the Cryptic's Discord server. Each puzzle is handcrafted and tested by the GAPP team. It is designed to require little to no prior experience to solve. The series is inspired by Genuinely Approachable Sudoku (GAS), a daily Sudoku series hosted on the same server.

Each puzzle comes with time standards — we reward a Speedy Sloth for solving a puzzle very quickly, a Quick Crab for solving a puzzle quickly, and anyone who solves a puzzle can claim a different bird every day!

Occasionally, we also give out otters for additional challenges.

Mind the GAPP

Mind the GAPP is a monthly compilation of every puzzle posted in the previous month. Each volume will also contain bonus puzzles that are of higher difficulty than the usual GAPP.
